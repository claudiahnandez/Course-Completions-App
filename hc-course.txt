<h3>Disaggregating by <b>Course</b> attributes</h3>
<center><img src="hc_c_layout.jpg" width=1000 heigth=250 ></center></br>
<ol><li><h4><b>Choose a specific time frame</b></h4></li>
<img src="time_frame_hc.jpg" width=400 heigth=200 align="left" hspace="30"></br>
  <ul>
	  <li>Select a year from the <strong>Academic Year</strong> drop down menu. If an academic year is not selected, it will default to the current academic year.</li>
	  <li>OPTIONAL: Select a <strong>Semester</strong> for the selected academic year to view data for a specific semester. If a semester is not selected, it will default to the academic year. Note: Data for the current 
	      semester is not available until final grades have been submitted.</li>
	</ul>
    
</br><li><h4><b>Specify Department, Discipline, and Course</b></h4> </li>    
<img src="departments_hc.jpg" width=800 heigth=100 hspace="30"><br/>
    <ul>
        <li>Select a <strong>Department</strong> from the drop down menu, otherwise it will default to "ALL". "ALL" means college-wide. Only one department maybe selected at a time. The departments listed are ones that have been offered in the selected <em>Academic Year</em> or <em>Semester</em>.</li>
	
	      <li>OPTIONAL: Select a <strong>Discipline</strong> from the drop down menu, otherwise it will default to "ALL". "ALL" means all disciplines within the
	        selected <em>Department</em>. Only one discipline may be selected at a time. The disciplines listed are ones that have been offered in the
	       selected <em>Academic Year</em> or <em>Semester</em> for the selected <em>Department</em>.</li>
	
	      <li>OPTIONAL: Select a <strong>Course</strong> from the drop down menu, otherwise it will default to "ALL". "ALL" means all courses within the selected 		
           <em>Discipline</em>. Only one course may be selected at a time. The courses listed are ones that have been offered in the selected&nbsp;<em>Academic Year</em> or <em>Semester</em> for the selected <em>Discipline</em>.</li>
    </ul>

</br>

<li><h4><strong>Click on the Historical Comparison tab</strong></h4></li>
<p>The color of the tab changes to white when selected</p>

</br>

<li><h4><strong>Click on the Student sub-tab to compare by student attributes</strong></h4></li>
<p>The color of the tab changes to white when selected.</p>

</br>

<li> <h4><b>Choose how many consecutive years or similar terms to compare</b></h4></li>
 <img src="comparison_years.jpg" width=400 heigth=500 hspace="30" align="left"></br>
  <ul>
	  <li>Select how many years/terms to view data for from the drop-down menu&nbsp;</li>

	  <li>Up to 5 years worth of data is available starting from the <i>Academic Year</i> or <i>Semester</i> specified in Step 1.</li>

	  <li>The default is 2 years/terms.</li>

	  <li>If viewing academic year data, then consecutive years will display.</li>

	  <li>If viewing term data, then similar terms will display (e.g., compare Fall to Fall)</li>
  </ul>
</br>


<li><h4><b>Choose a category to filter by</b></h4></li>
<img src="hc_c_options.jpg" width=900 heigth=400 hspace="30">
   <ul>
      <li>There are 3 available categories: <b>Class Type</b>, <b>Location</b>, and <b>Day & Time</b>. These categories are only available in the "Historical
          Comparison" tab.</li>
      
      <li><i>Options available under each category depend on what Department, Discipline and/or Course is selected</i></li>
      
      <li>Comparisons are only available within each category. Once a sub-group is selected, subgroups from all other categories will be grayed out.</li>

        <ol style="list-style-type: lower-roman;">
           <li><i>For example, if Main Campus is selected, then only the other location sub-groups will be available for selection, and the Class Type
                  and Start Time & Day categories will be grayed out.</i></li>
        </ol>
    
      <li>To use the Start Time & Day category, click on the Add Day button. Select the day(s) from the drop down menu and corresponding times
         from the drop down menu. A maximum of 3 days may be added.</li>
      <ol style="list-style-type: lower-roman;">
           <li><i>To remove a day, click on the Remove Day button.The Remove Day
                  button will be grayed out when it reaches the minimum of one day, which is the default.</i></li>
           <li><i>If the Days and Times are not properly selected, an error message will appear.</i></li>
      </ol>

   </ul>

</br>
<li><h4><strong>Click Filter</strong></h4></li>
 <img src="hc_buttons.jpg" width=250 heigth=80 hspace="30" alight="right">
  <p>Click on <b>Filter</b> to submit the request based on the filters specified in Steps 1 to 6.</p>
  <p>The <b>Clear</b> button clears the filters from Step 6.</p>
      
</ol>
